ALL GENERATED OUTPUTS MUST ADHERE TO THIS BIBLE WITHOUT EXCEPTION.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CORE AESTHETIC
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every asset must feel:
  • cinematic
  • premium
  • emotionally immersive
  • moody
  • filmic
  • atmospheric
  • realistic (photoreal where appropriate)
  • artistically curated (as if from a festival-level film or AAA game cinematic)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STRICTLY FORBIDDEN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  ✗ Generic AI look / plastic skin / waxy textures
  ✗ Repetitive compositions or safe centered framing
  ✗ Fake HDR glow, oversaturated neon, "epic" over-lighting
  ✗ Cyberpunk overload unless explicitly for a cyber world asset
  ✗ Oversharpening / crunchy edges / halo artifacts
  ✗ Low-detail rendering / muddy backgrounds / flat lighting
  ✗ AI slop aesthetics: extra limbs, bad anatomy, text artifacts, warped faces
  ✗ Generic poster layouts (centered hero + floating title + 3 floating heads)
  ✗ Stock-photo cleanliness or corporate gloss

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TECHNICAL QUALITY BAR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  • Cinematic lighting (practical sources, motivated shadows, volumetric god rays where appropriate)
  • Film grain or subtle digital film emulation (35mm / Alexa / RED look)
  • Rich color grading appropriate to emotional tone (never generic teal-orange unless story-driven)
  • Deep depth of field control or intentional shallow focus
  • Environmental storytelling in every frame
  • Faces and bodies must pass human anatomy + emotional micro-expression check
  • No text on image unless it is diegetic (signage, screens, tattoos) and legible

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EMOTIONAL MATCHING — PROMPT REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Psychological Horror
  → oppressive, isolated, dread-filled, cinematic darkness, negative space, 
    practical low-key lighting, decaying or sterile environments, 
    sense of being watched, slow suffocation of hope

Emotional Drama
  → intimate, human, warm but melancholic, lived-in spaces, 
    natural window light or practical lamps, subtle emotional tension 
    in posture and eyes, rain on glass, quiet moments

Dark Sci-Fi
  → atmospheric, mysterious, volumetric light shafts, deep space or 
    grounded near-future, tactile technology, vast scale vs human frailty, 
    rich blacks, subtle color accents (sodium, cyan, amber)

Music Worlds / Ambient
  → dreamlike, emotionally rhythmic, soft focus or crystalline detail, 
    synesthetic color response to imagined sound, vast internal landscapes, 
    floating elements, memory-like haze, elegant minimalism

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
QUALITY FILTER (AUTOMATIC REJECTION CRITERIA)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Automatically reject and move to /rejected-assets if ANY of the following:
  • Malformed anatomy (hands, faces, limbs)
  • Muddy or flat lighting with no cinematic contrast
  • Repetitive / boring framing (dead center hero with nothing else happening)
  • Low-detail rendering (especially backgrounds or fabric)
  • Generic poster composition
  • Inconsistent cinematic quality with previous approved assets
  • Visible AI artifacts (text gibberish, melting shapes, extra fingers)

ONLY assets that survive this filter move to /approved-assets.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ASSET NAMING CONVENTION (ENFORCED)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Format:
  {category}_{emotional-key}_{descriptor}_{YYYYMMDDTHHMMSSZ}_{shortid}.{ext}

Examples:
  movie-poster_psychological-horror_the-hollowing_20260526T003000Z_k9p2m4.png
  rail-thumbnail_dark-sci-fi_veil-of-static_20260526T003500Z_x7v3q1.jpg
  creator-banner_music-world_elias-voss_20260526T004000Z_b2n8r5.png

Short ID = 6 char lowercase alphanumeric for uniqueness.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
METADATA REQUIREMENTS (EVERY APPROVED ASSET)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  • title (human cinematic title)
  • creator_identity (e.g. "Movianx Originals" or specific creator world)
  • emotional_category (from style bible)
  • recommendation_tags (array, for rails & discovery)
  • genre (primary)
  • atmospheric_profile (short descriptive string)
  • rail_assignment (e.g. "horror-rails", "featured-home-hero", "music-worlds-rail")
  • prompt_signature (for deduping)
  • source_file (relative path in factory)
  • approved_at
  • quality_notes (why it passed)
